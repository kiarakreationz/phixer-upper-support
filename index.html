pHixer Upper

ABG Interpretation & Medical Education

App Support

Welcome to pHixer Upper support! Our app helps medical students, nursing students, and healthcare professionals learn arterial blood gas (ABG) interpretation through comprehensive analysis tools and educational content.

Features

  • Comprehensive ABG Analysis: Advanced interpretation using Winter's formula and Henderson-Hasselbalch equation
  • Clinical Decision Support: ARDSnet Protocol integration with ventilator recommendations
  • Educational Excellence: Interactive compensation explanations with detailed formulas
  • Advanced Tools: Strong Ion Gap (SIG) calculations and metabolic acidosis analysis

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app for clinical use?

No. pHixer Upper is designed exclusively for educational purposes. It should not be used for patient diagnosis or treatment decisions.

Who is this app for?

This app is perfect for:

  • Medical students learning ABG interpretation
  • Nursing students in critical care programs
  • Respiratory therapy students
  • Healthcare educators and instructors

Does the app collect my data?

No. pHixer Upper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations are performed locally on your device.

What iOS version do I need?

pHixer Upper requires iOS 16.0 or later.
